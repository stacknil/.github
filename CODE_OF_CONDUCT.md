# Code of Conduct

## Standard

This project space is maintained for constructive, technically grounded collaboration.

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, intimidation, doxxing, and clearly bad-faith behavior are not accepted.

## Expected behavior

- Be specific and evidence-based.
- Critique ideas, code, design, and decisions rather than attacking people.
- Respect repository scope, boundaries, and maintainer guidance.
- Keep discussion relevant, professional, and reproducible where possible.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abuse
- Hate speech or identity-based attacks
- Publishing private or sensitive information
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Repeated low-effort hostility after correction

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content, and may restrict participation when needed to protect the project space.

## Reporting

If a conduct issue overlaps with safety, privacy, or security concerns, follow the contact path described in `SECURITY.md` or `SUPPORT.md`.
